Honey-Garlic Slow Cooker Chicken Thighs

Description

Super simple garlic and honey-glazed chicken thighs. Serve with rice or roasted vegetables.

Ingredients

    • 4 skinless, boneless chicken thigh
      1/2 c. soy sauce
      1/2 c. ketchup
      1/3 c. honey
      3 cloves garlic, minced
      1 tsp. dried basil

    • Steps

    • 1. Lay chicken thighs into the bottom of a 4-quart slow cooker.
      2. Whisk soy sauce, ketchup, honey, garlic, and basil together in a bowl; pour over the chicken.
      3. Cook on Low for 6 hours.
